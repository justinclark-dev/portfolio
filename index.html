My developer story

I'm originally a self-taught programmer, and over the years I’ve learned many new technologies and programming languages, enough so that I can get up to speed pretty quickly on any new technology or language. Still, I feel that to be the best developer I can be, and to be competitive, it’s really best to have a well-rounded education that is complete with a degree. So, currently I'm a full-time student and will finish my Associate's Degree in Programming this fall so I can get back to work. After that I will start working on my Bachelor’s Degree in Application Development here at NSC, which is thankfully a night program.

I love challenges

As with anything, developing websites and web applications takes time and patience as there are always new challenges to overcome. But that’s one of the main reasons I love being a developer, that and making cool stuff. So, I constantly struggle with new challenges in order to grow.
